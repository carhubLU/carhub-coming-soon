About CarHub

CarHub is a platform that allows drivers to easily book garage services and stay connected to the car world across Luxembourg and the Greater Region.

Our goal is to simplify the entire experience — from finding the right garage to managing services in a fast and intuitive way.

CarHub will also bring together local automotive events and relevant information, creating one central place for everything related to your car.

We are currently building the first version of CarHub across mobile and web.

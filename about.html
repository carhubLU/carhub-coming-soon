About CarHub

CarHub is a platform designed to simplify how drivers interact with garages, service providers and other vehicle care businesses.

Our goal is to make booking vehicle-related services fast, transparent and straightforward, allowing users to find and arrange services such as garages, tyre changes, car washes, detailing, paint protection film and more with ease.

In addition to bookings, CarHub brings together what is happening in the automotive world across Luxembourg and the Greater Region, including events, meet-ups and relevant updates for car enthusiasts.

By combining practical tools with community-focused content, CarHub aims to become a central hub for drivers, whether they are managing their vehicle or staying connected to the local automotive scene.

CarHub is currently in development and will be launching soon.
